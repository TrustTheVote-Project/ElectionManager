Jurisdictions

Most election related data in ElectionManager is part of a single Jurisdiction. Elections, Candidates, Contests, Questions, all of these relate back to the Jurisdiction that they belong to. Some states refer to these as Counties or other local terminology.

Most prominently, you can find the list of Elections here. An Election is a single event for a specific date. Ballots are generated for a specific Election, for example.
