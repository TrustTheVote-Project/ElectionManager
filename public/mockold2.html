Assets

Election Manager centrally stores the images and large blocks of text that are used in ballots. This way it is easy to see what is already available, and upload or delete other assets

All assets are identified by an all important key called the "ident". These idents are used for example in ballot style sheets or other places to refer to an asset of a logo, or an instructional diagram, or any other uploaded files.

This section of Election Manager is used to manage these assets.
